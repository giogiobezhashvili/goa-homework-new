The Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupé is an executive car (E-segment) introduced in 2018 by Mercedes-AMG.
It is marketed as a five-door variant of the AMG GT two-door sports car.
Despite the name and style, the GT 4-Door Coupé is closely related to the E 63 AMG with its performance chassis, and is featured by the AMG-specific rear frame and a different front axle for better kinematics.
